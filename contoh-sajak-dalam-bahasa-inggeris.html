17. contoh report text dalam bahasa Inggeris tentang banjir


Flood

Floods are natural disasters that often occur in many cities on different scales where excessive amounts of water are on normally dry soil. According to KBBI or an Indonesian dictionary, the definition of flood is runny and too heavy, sometimes overflowing. That can occur because of the amount of water in lakes, rivers, or other water catchments that exceed normal capacity due to accumulation of rainwater or compression so that it spills.

When floods occur, many people lose their property. It even causes casualties. Therefore, it is better to know the causes of flooding in order to take appropriate steps to prevent flooding. The first cause is illegal logging. Illegal logging makes the forest barren one of the causes of flooding. This is because tree roots have the function of absorbing water. Therefore, if many trees are lost, it will easily flood. The second cause is littering. The cause of this one flood is no stranger. Randomly disposed of waste dumped in rivers or other waterways can clog the water flow so that it can overflow and cause flooding.

The third cause is settlements on the banks of rivers or rivers. Settlements are built on the banks of rivers which are vulnerable to siltation. Siltation that occurs in rivers because of the habit of throwing garbage into the river and the condition of the soil on both sides of the building can collapse and then close the river side. So that the river becomes narrower and prone to flooding. The fourth cause is lowland. Areas located in the lowlands can cause flooding. That's because flood water flows from the highlands to the lowlands so that it can be at risk of flooding. The fifth cause is high rainfall. The cause of the flood was caused by weather conditions. The cause of the seven floods is that the dam was violated. The results can result in a very adverse flash flood. The eight causes are one of the spatial management systems. The cause of this flood can cause water that is difficult to absorb and flow slowly.
