As quoted by the World Health Organization (WHO), the Corona virus originates from Coronaviruses (CoV) which causes diseases ranging from the common cold to more severe ones such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS-CoV) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS-CoV). Whereas for Coronavirus Novel (nCoV) is a new type that has not been previously identified in humans.

Corona virus is a zoonosis, meaning that it is transmitted between animals and humans. According to investigations that have been carried out, SARS-CoV is transmitted from civet cats or better known as weasels to humans and MERS-CoV from camels to humans. However, some Corona viruses are also known to circulate in animals that have not previously infected humans.
